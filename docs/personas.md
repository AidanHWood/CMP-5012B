# Personas

## Persona 1: Kieran Corbet
- Age: 35
- Background: Married father with 7 children and has become more health-conscious as he is feeling out of breath trying to keep up with his kids and finds himself eating a little too much chocolate.
- Goals: Get fitter to be able to spend more time with the kids.
- Frustrations: Is a habitual snacker, struggles to find time between working and looking after the kids to exercise.
- Typical scenario of use: To track what he eats in a day to try and build healthier habits around diet and setting achievable health goals without feeling overwhelmed.

## Persona 2: Leonard Fletcher
- Age: 19
- Background: Single man focused on self-improvement and creating fitness content as an influencer.
- Goals: Further improve upon his already athletic physique and keep up motivation to keep training.
- Frustrations: Is starting to be complacent about his fitness as his health data is spread across several apps and he feels he has "met" his health goals.
- Typical scenario of use: Daily user to log in, track calories and workouts.

## Persona 3: Olivia Mcnaney
- Age: 23
- Background: Single woman scared to train alone and deflective when questioned about her health and fitness goals.
- Goals: Tracking health and competing with her friends to stay motivated and healthy.
- Frustrations: Struggles to find motivation and hold herself accountable.
- Typical scenario of use: To track her health goals and check on her friends doing the same.
