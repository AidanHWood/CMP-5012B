# Project Vision

## 1. Background
Many people want to improve their health but stuggle to understand their daily lifestyle habits in a consistent way. Information about exercise, diet, weight and personal goals is often recorded using multiple tools / apps, making it difficult to track progress. This can lead to a reduction in motivation and unclear and unrealistic health goals. A simple system is needed to help users record key health information and reflect on their progress overtime, giving them the confidence in making informed and sustainable lifestyle decisions.

## 2. Project Goals
- Goal 1: Provide a simple and accessable way for users to record exercise, diet, and weight.
- Goal 2: Enable users to set realistic health goals and monitor the progress towards them.
- Goal 3: Allow users to access their histroical health information in a clear and understandable format.
- Goal 4: Support user motivation thorugh feedback and progress tracking.

## 3. Primary Users & Stakeholders
- Primary Users: Individuals who want to monitor their personal health, fitness, and lifestyle habits.
- Secondary Users: Users interested in sharing progress thorugh groups or challenges.
- Other stakeholders: Health and wellness organisations, insurers, and system maintainers.

## 4. Success Criteria
- Criterion 1: Users can successfully create a profile and record health data without assistance.
- Criterion 2: Users can view clear trends and history that help them understand progress over time.
- Criterion 3: Users recieve meaningful feedback when health goals are met or missed.
